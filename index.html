Antonio's Project Portfolio

Welcome to my website! Here are the two big projects I worked on this year.

Our Shared Canvas: Screenprinting Project

What I Did and Learned

In this project, I learned how to do screenprinting. I helped create a design, put it onto a mesh screen, and used a squeegee to push the colorful ink through onto the frame. I learned that you have to be very careful and patient so the ink doesn't smudge and ruin the design.

My 21st Century Skill

The skill I showed the most growth in was Collaboration (Working Together). I had to work closely with my partners to hold the screen totally still and make sure our designs lined up perfectly. We communicated well to make sure nobody made a mistake.

Biotech Breakthroughs: Genetics Experiment

What I Did and Learned

In this project, we did an awesome genetics experiment in the lab. We studied DNA and looked at how different traits are passed down from parents to kids. I learned how scientists can look at tiny genes to understand how living things grow and change. That race doesn't decide your genes

My 21st Century Skill

The skill I used the most was Critical Thinking (Problem Solving). When we were looking at our science data, it didn't look right at first. I had to double-check our lab steps, look closely at what we did, and figure out how to fix the problem.
